Fueling the Passion

Celebrating 5 Years (2021-2026)

Established in 2021, Axela Kenya (AK) began with a handful of passionate drivers and has rapidly grown into the nation's premier community for Mazda 3 and Axela enthusiasts.

We are more than just a car club; we're a brotherhood defined by road trips, high-energy car meets, and a shared admiration for precision engineering. Welcome to a culture driven by movement and mechanical perfection.
